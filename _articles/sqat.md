[SQAT](https://sqat.farm/) is a 3.5-year Horizon Europe project implementing smart farming applications using Earth-observation data, soil sensors and robotics &mdash; tagline: *&ldquo;Laboratory In The Field&rdquo;*. The consortium is coordinated by the Association of Balkan Eco&ndash;Innovation and brings together 11 partners, including [ILVO](https://ilvo.vlaanderen.be/) on the Belgian agronomic side and Exobotic on the robotic-sampling side.

I was involved in the **preliminary conception**, the **design of the sampling tools**, and in **securing Exobotic&rsquo;s place in the consortium** during the grant-writing phase.
