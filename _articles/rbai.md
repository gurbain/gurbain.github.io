## Areas of expertise

- **Embedded systems**:  20+ years of hands-on work with diverse hardware platforms, Embedded Linux, IoT and communication protocols.
- **Machine vision**:  2D and 3D cameras, Lidar, with object detection, segmentation and classification.
- **Advanced robotics**:  robot control and integration, with a focus on AGVs, legged robots and robotic arms.
- **Artificial intelligence**:  data science, deep learning and generative AI applied to industrial problems.
- **Tooling &amp; methodology**:  Python, C/C++, Docker, Git, Linux, cloud platforms, and an Agile-flavoured workflow adapted to hardware.
